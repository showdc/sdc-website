Bangkok’s first integrated indoor and outdoor convention, exhibition and entertainment venue.

the ShowDC
Venue Experience

We are an all-in one event facility consisting of a high-tech multipurpose performance hall, an outdoor arena and a tourist lounge. Our innovative spaces can be transformed to suit a wide range of events and festivals from Broadway shows, K-Pop concerts, corporate events, meetings and seminars to crowd pleasing exhibitions and epic Electronic Dance Music festivals. Let our creative and engaged team take you on a venue experience like no other. We take an active approach in servicing our guests and thrive on introducing fresh ideas, anticipating and responding to the evolving needs of a constantly changing world.

  • Banquets
  • Concert
  • Outdoor Event

Five star facilities combined with access to a wide range of first-class catering options should make The SHOW DC Venue Experience your first choice as a banquet venue. Able to entertain up to 1,600 guests, our team is prepared for the biggest and most prestigious of events and will ensure a seamless banquet experience.

The minimalist modern décor of the The SHOW DC performance hall has been acoustically treated to create a world-class venue and sound for concerts and has all of the backstage and front of house facilities required to host leading Thai and international artists of any calibre in an impressive 6,000 capacity hall.

With unparalleled accessibility for an outdoor performance space of this size, the Oasis Arena will appeal to event organizers looking for an exciting venue that is within reach of everyone. From Songkran parties to international concerts by bands such as Two Door Cinema Club, the Oasis Arena is already considered one of Bangkok’s leading outdoor venues.
