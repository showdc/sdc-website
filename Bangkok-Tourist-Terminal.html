Experiences

T make sure that your journey to Thailand both begins and ends well, make Bangkok Tourist Terminal the first and last stop on your holiday itinerary. Experience the famous SHOW DChospitality with facilities that go far beyond even the best airport terminals. Everything you need before and after your flight is on hand, whether its somewhere to relax and gather your thoughts ora last dash tobuy presents for loved ones, you can find everything all under one roof.

Beyond the shops, SHOW DC offers fine dining with a wide range of cuisines at our many restaurants and if you have the time, some amazing entertainment with live performances and shows across the complex.

If you’ve just arrived let us show you the way with tourist help and a wealth of trips and tours to choose from – everything you need to make for a wonderful journey in the land of smiles.

Shuttle Services & Popular Domestic Trips

Bangkok Bus Terminal is your gateway to Thailand with a range of shuttle services, tours and trips for you to choose from. The many fascinating destinations in Bangkok and across the country arewaiting to be explored with our comfortable and convenient services ready to take you to another world.

In the land of smiles, the journey isas important as the destination and the sightseeing begins as soon as our luxury shuttle bus service leaves SHOW DC. Experience the vibrancy of everyday city life as you pass it by or venture further afield to upcountry towns and seaside resorts.

Joint Tour

Various Joint tours to the Grand Palace, Floating Market, Ayuthaya Tour etc.. will depart from SHOW DC. You can take the free shuttle service from 30 Bangkok hotels to SHOW DC and depart for your tour from there. Before and after your tour, you can enjoy various attractions at SHOW DC and also return back to your hotel or the airport with the free hotel and airport shuttle services.

Various Tourist Attraction Shuttle Service

If you have had trouble using Taxis or Public transportation to get from your hotel to Bangkok tourist attractions then don't worry, All of the famous tourist attraction companies are running shuttle services to and from SHOW DC.

Wonderful Pearl Cruise, Dream World, Rose Garden, Lumpini Academy Thai Boxing, Safari World etc..
