About lotte duty free (Thailand)

World-Class shopping experience

With 13 branches spanning across 4 countries and over 850 international brands, Lotte Duty Free is the largest duty free shop operator in Asia and the third largest in the global duty free business. The Lotte Duty Free (Thailand) at SHOW DC marks the company’s giant leap into the world stage with its first store in the world’s rising tourism and retail hub - Bangkok.

Indulge in a wide range of brands

From the most desirable luxuary boutiques to selected local stores and your favorite offerings from Korea.

Where to find us?

You can come and enjoy the duty-free shopping via the K-District entrance or at the front of the Lotte entrance. From there just walk up the escalators towards Lotte Duty Free on the 2nd and 3rd floors

If you come in from the Main Entrance to the Atrium then walk to the Lotte Wing which is on the right side.

Don’t forget to meet and greet with your favourite K-Idols gallery at Lotte Star Avenue before starting your exotic duty free shopping experience.

SUPER JUNIOR Zone

The Super Junior Zone features merchandise and pictures from one of Korea’s biggest boy bands. Get closer to the boys that brought you hits like “Miracle” and "Sorry, Sorry”.

2PM ZONE

Thai fans of K-Pop worship 2PM for their memorable songs such as “Heartbeat”. This is their chance to buy the latest 2PM merchandise and learn more about each member through the interactive displays.

PHOTO ZONE - KIM SOO HYUN

The next best thing to a selfie with Kim Soo Hyun can be taken with Kim’s wax model. Star of the fantasy romance series My Love from the Star, he has already won the hearts of Thai fans and is waiting for this great photo opportunity.

PHOTO ZONE - LEE MIN HO

Impress your friends with a photo of you and the hottest K-Actor in the business. The lifelike model of Lee Min Ho is the closest Thai fans can get to their hero without heading to an expensive meet and greet.

K-STARS ZONE

There are so many stars in Korea’s galaxy and the K-Stars zone will introduce you to celebrities, singers and actors not featured elsewhere on Star Avenue.

EXO ZONE

Some say that Exo are the biggest boyband in the world. For their fans this has never been in doubt and they can find the latest merchandise and pictures of the boys in the Exo zone.

STAR ROOM

The Star Room features collections of memorabilia from Exo, Twice and Kim Soo Hyun along with the latest pictures of these leading K-Idols.

MOVING WIDECOLOUR

See your favourite K-Idols, larger than life and enhanced on Star Avenue’s Moving Widecolour screen. The detail of this new visual technology will bring you closer to your favourite stars.
