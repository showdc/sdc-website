Parking Services


SHOW DC provides a secure parking facility optimised for our customers. To make things easier we have a Car Park Assist and a Numberplate Recognition system that is state-of-the-art. There are approximately 1200 parking spaces for cars and vans. And 100 parking spaces for tourist buses.

Parking Lot Operating Times:

General Zone

Underground floors, 2nd and 3rd floor
06:30 am - 10:00 pm

Entertainment Zone

Underground floors, 2nd and 3rd floor
9:30 am - 2:00 am

Parking Fee

Enjoy up to 3 hours free parking at any one of the car parks in SHOW DC. If you park for longer than 3 hours, you will need to pay for the subsequence hours.

  • Fist 3 hours are free
  • If more than 3 hours, the charge is at 30 Baht per hour.
  • In case your parking ticket is damaged or lost, you will have to pay fines of 500 baht per card. Excludes parking fee at 30 baht per hour and must provide proof of ownership.

Rules

The management at SHOW DC reserves the right to amend the terms of oarking rules at any time.

  • SHOW DC reserves the right not to allow cars over 2 meters to park in the building.
  • Car parking is available for customers from 6:30 am - 10:30 pm. However, entertainment zone customers are requested to park within the designated area, as this area parking operates until 2:00 am.
  • No overnight parking.
  • SHOW DC reserves the right to take any action based on appropiration. Whether it is a requerst for a police officer for a warning to enforce the user to comply therules within the parking lot.
  • SHOW DC's management provide the parking lot for the sole purpose to enable visitor to park the car. Management is not responsible for damage to or loss of vehicles entering the building.
  • Parking space is not allow for any driving training and other non-related activities which may disrub the operation of the parking lot.
  • Do not repair, assemble, or clean the car inside the parking lot.
