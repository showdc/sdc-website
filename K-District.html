Feel the pulses and beats of Seoul’s best-loved shopping streets in the heart of Bangkok.One of a kind shopping experience for the die-hard devotees of all things Korean and a fun-loving shoppers who are in eternal search for exciting shopping destinations, K- District puts together a carefully-curated selection of latest, trend-setting K-Fashion, K-Beauty and K-Food, and bring them into this whole new, exhilarating atmosphere.

Dress up in style with
the latest trends in K-Fashion

Uncover the latest must-haves and trends from your favourite Korean super stars. Hand picked by some of Korea’s top stylists, we bring to Bangkok’s fashionistas and hardcore shoppers, the latest and hottest designer brands from Korea at Current Fashion Seoul Select Shop.

Current Fashion Seoul

Dress yourself in the latest looks from Korea’s young designers at Current Fashion Seoul Select Shop. Mix and match your selection to create your very own distinct fashion trend.

The Hottest Fashion Brands

Discover the K-pop Stars
Glamorous Beauty Secrets

In an exclusive partnership with Korea’s Top Beauty TV Show “Get It Beauty,” BKK’s Hongdae boasts hundreds of shelves filled with latest beauty crazes and make-up secrets from the one of world’s most influential capitals of beauty. Another key attraction is the Korean Beauty School where you can test and try latest beauty and make-up trends from Korea before everyone else!

Current Beauty Seoul

Discover your favourite Korean beauty brands to match your style. Enjoy shopping where you can test and try the latest beauty and make up trends from Korea right in the heart of Bangkok. Current Beauty Seoul - a shopping paradise where every girl’s dream comes true.

We love modern and chic K-lifestyle

A lifestyle store that provides modern and practical daily use products with good design and chicness.

Current Lifestyle Seoul

Current Lifestyle Seoul brings you innovatively designed lifestyle products including kid-art, star goods, stationery, gadgets, travel goods and home decor. Grab some today and add some colour into your daily life.

K-Celebrity Street Food

K-Celeb Restaurants &
K-Street Foods

This is a place where you can experience the best of Korea through our authentic K-Celeb Restaurants and K-Street Food stores. A unique F&B brand experience that features famous Korean celebrities and reenacts the popular vibe of Myeongdong’s Food Street.

K-Celebrity Street Food

Feast on the taste of Korea and authentic flavours of your favourite Korean dishes or simply indulge in the largest selection of Korean produce and household culinary names at the K-Super.

Hearty Curated Brands

Exclusive to K District and a world first, the K-Celeb restaurants and cafes are a unique way for fans to get closer to their idols whilst sharing their taste in food and drinks. Hang out and enjoy a wide array of Korean and international cuisines at unique eateries created by stars such as Psy, Rain, BTS, Seung-Ji, Kim Sung-Su, Lee Seo-Jin and Super Junior’s Choi Si Won.

PSY
Korean Noodle House
Have your noodles Gangnum style at Psy Noodle House. The creator of the iconic dance move presents his twist on Korea’s classic dish with a “K Ramen BAR” that features some of his favourites. Ramen is featured heavily on the menu with Traditional Spicy Korean noodle cooked with manila clam, anchovy, or sunny plants stock, Squid Jjamppong plus Shin, Blue Crab or Shrimp Ramen. For those that prefer their noodles stir fried there is a choice of Black Soybean Stir-Fried Ramen, Jjappaguri, Spicy mixed noodle – all topped off with a diverse selection of Korean noodle toppings such as Dumplings, Rice Cake, Cheese and Bean Sprouts.
KIM’S FULL HOUSE

Star of hit Korean television show Full House, Kim Sung-Su has created a place to celebrate the country’s healthiest and most popular vegetable. Kim’s Full House uses only the very best traditionally ripened Kimchi, served in a bowl with fried rice and a variety of toppings such as chicken, egg, shrimp, tofu and spicy pork.

Kimchi can also be enjoyed as a snack to go with Kimchi Fried Rice rolled in a tortilla – a healthy and tasty reminder of why the average Korean eats over 18kg of Kimchi every year!

AFTER THE RAIN
Rain is coming…to brunch! Named by Time Magazine as one of the "100 Most Influential People Who Shape Our World", singer, songwriter and actor Rain is now ready to conquer you stomachs with his After The Rain Brunch Café. The all day brunch is made with only the freshest and healthiest ingredients and features such delights as baguette french toast, Huevos Rancheros, ricotta pancake, corn fritters or Egg Jam toast. The rest of the menu reflects Rain’s love of Italian food with specialties from southern Italy inclucing pasta made with rich seafood, Vongole, king prawn tomato linguine, Carbonara and Abalone asparagus linguine. The wood oven at the café provides a steady supply of authentic and fusion pizzas whilst desserts are Koreans’ favorite sweet breads with fillings and crumbles.
SEUNG-GI
SLOPPY MILL
Leading singer, actor, host and entertainer, Lee Seung-Gi presents K Street food at its finest with Koreans’ favorite snacks: Ddukbokki and Hodduk rice cakes. These sweet and savoury treats come with the traditional choice of sauces and toppings, dripping in spicy or soy sauce or with Ramen noodles and with toppings such as Gimmari, fried Calamari or shrimp, fish cakes and flat dumpling. Those with a sweet tooth should try the Sloppy Hodduk roasted with additional sweet rice dough as well as the seed, glass noodles, black sesame or mango Hodduk.
CHOI SI WONBUGSY DOG
Super Junior’s Choi Si Won has given the American classic a Korean makeover, taking the humble Hot Dog to new levels of flavourful harmony between bun and sausage. The toppings are inspired by Korean and other Asian cuisines with a taste unique to Bugsy Dog. Try a Kimchi Chili Dog, Bahn mi Dog or the fusion of Isaan and America that is the Som Tam Dog. To wash down this feast, beverages include K-sodas, Lemonade and Omija Sparkling - a great match with these delicious hotdogs
LEE SEO-JINSAMSICOCO
Popular Korean reality TV show, Three Meals A Day saw Lee Seo-Jin try his hand at growing, harvesting and cooking his own food. Whilst admittedly a poor cook at first, through the TV show he has grown to love cooking and now wants to share his love of chicken wings with the Samsicoco Wing House. The simple menu is alive with flavor and features wings marinated in several different ways to a secret recipe and served with taste-bud tingling dips – all ready to be shared and enjoyed with friends.

Enjoy Korean cuisine from the comfort of home

Get authentic Korean ingredients from general produce to groceries to meats and seafood right here at the K-Market. We will bring you never before seen variety and freshness direct from the source.

K-Super

Our strength is in working directly with local vendors and farmers to bring to our stores only products of the highest standards and quality straight from Korea. Allow our Korean food experts to help you plan your next home cooked meal.

The shopper dream comes true!
The One Stop Korean Shopping Destination

Where to find us?

The Main Entrance is on the 1st floor of the side of the building. Once inside you will see the Atrium and you can walk along the right hand side of the Lotte Wing to eat at Celebrity restaurants and enjoy your favourite idol’s gallery on Lotte Star Avenue. Alternatively, from the Atrium you can start along the left-hand side before reaching the Bangkok Tourist and then Korean Street food restaurants and enjoy one-of-a-kind shopping experience of Current Fashion Seoul.
