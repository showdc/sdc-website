Discover the delicacies, souvenirs and treasures of Thailand ALL IN ONE PLACE

Thai Market is a one- stop destination for those looking for something beyond souvenirs and memorabilia. Discover everything from unique food, sweets and ingredients to create flavourful Thai cuisine along with authentic handicrafts and artisanal pieces.

Fresh Fruit

Thailand’s orchards provide a bountiful harvest throughout the year with each new season bringing mouth-watering delights. Working together with local communities and farmers across the country, Thai Thai Market brings together the freshest produce at Thailand’s largest fruit market, packed with a wide variety of produce from ramubutan and mangosteen to pineapple and coconuts. As well as seasonal fruits, you can enjoy the “King of Fruits” Durian and delicious varieties of mango all year round. There is no better place on earth to enjoy the fruits of the earth than Thai Thai Market at Show DC – a gateway to the verdant orchards of Thailand and the tropical delights they produce.

Grocery

Discover the secrets of Thai cuisine with a tantalizing range of herbs, spices and seasonings to create the perfect meal. At Thai Thai Market’s grocery section you will find easy to prepare, ready to cook meals that remove the need for the arduous preparation that goes into Thai cooking whilst delivering the classic taste enjoyed in restaurants and homes across the country.

Thai people also love to eat between meals with snacks such as prawn crackers, dried seaweed and other dried fruit like banana, mango and durian with unique taste that cannot find in general. Whether you are looking to snack, cook or for a souvenir of your stay then what better way than to take a taste of Thailand back home with you.

Selected Thai Taste

Thai Thai Market has selected only the tastiest and finest quality street-food, desserts and snacks from all four corners of the kingdom, bringing them together under one roof for you to experience and enjoy. These Thai snacks and desserts also make the perfect gift or souvenir and are great to share with friends and family. Among the many mouthwatering tastes are Kalamae (Thai style caramel), Thong Muan (coconut crispy rolls), Mhee Grob (crispy noodles) or banana baked in honey. Whatever dessert or snack you choose, the only challenge will be getting them home to your loved ones without eating them all yourself.

DELICATESEN

Whilst Thai cuisine has travelled the world and found international fame, the country’s sweets and snacks are an under-rated secret waiting to be discovered. As any walk down a Bangkok street will tell you, you’re never far away from Thai desserts and snacks, usually signified by delicious aromas on the breeze. There are so many different sweet delights to try and at Thai Thai Market you can find them all under one roof. With such a wide variety to choose from, this is the ideal place to try something exotic and new such as Insect Snacks - it was one of animals economy in Northeast, the most famous is l’Sadigg or Cricket with the uniqueness sour and creamy taste. Those with more traditional tastes can indulge in one of the classics such as Kanom Krok, Thai-style donuts, sticky rice and mango or coconut ice cream. If Durian is king of the fruits then sticky rice and mango is the king of desserts!

Arts & Crafts

Thailand is famous for exquisitely made handicrafts and fabrics and has been known around the world in silk industry and the many tourists that have visited the country and shared taken home handicrafts over the past few decades. All are amazed at the intricate work and attention to detail considering the affordable prices. These handicrafts not only make for special gifts or mementos of your visit to Thailand, they also help sustain communities across the country.

Beauty, Health
& Spas

Knowledge of the healing and therapeutic properties of plants, herbs and other natural products have been passed down through the generations over thousands of years. This local wisdom continues to be refined and improved, helping to relax and alleviate stress and pain around the world in the form of a wide range of health, beauty and spa products. From luxurious oils and soaps to the simple healing properties of traditional Thai medicines such as Ya-mhong (Tiger Balm) and Yaa-dhom (a mentholated inhaler) you will find everything you need for a healthy and beautiful body.
