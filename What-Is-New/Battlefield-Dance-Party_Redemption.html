BATTLE FIELD Dance Party Tickets Redemption Date 29 May – 9 June 2017

 

 
The Hottest Party at the Biggest Dance “Battle Field Dance Party”. Exclusively for SHOW DC customer, get concert tickets for free with the following promotions.

Terms & Conditions for BATTLE FIELD Dance Party

  1. Collect receipts worth more than 3,499 THB from any stores in SHOW DC, get free 2 tickets of BATTLE FIELD Dance Party Concert
  2. Receipts can be collected during 29 May – 9 June 2017 only.
  3. Receipts that are already used for ticket redemption cannot be reused with other promotion campaign.
  4. Tickets or receipts from Himmapan Avatar and Ongbak LIVE can be accepted only those issued by Thaiticketmajor.
  5. Receipts from Food Tradition cannot be used in this promotion campaign.
  6. Written cash receipts cannot be used in this promotion campaign.
  7. Limited the ticket redemption only 1 person (under the same name, surname, and citizen ID) per 2 tickets throughout the campaign.
  8. Tickets can be redeem during 29 May – 9 June 2017, or until stocks run out. 
  9. Customer needs to present ID card and original receipts at the Thaiticketmajor Counter in Tourist Lounge, 1st Floor, everyday from 10.00 am. - 10.00 pm.
  10. SHOW DC reserves the right to distribute the tickets on first come first serve basis. 
  11. SHOW DC reserves the right to change the conditions without prior notice. In case of any dispute, SHOW DC decision is final. 
  12. Customer participating in this activity have read and well understood, and agrees to all the terms and conditions mentioned above. 
  13. Staff and family of SHOW DC, its subsidiaries, participating stores in this promotion are not allowed to participate in this promotion campaign.
