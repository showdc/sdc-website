Shuttle Bus Service : ONEREPUBLIC LIVE IN BANGKOK

- Customer can purchase one way shuttle bus ticket to event venue 

Round trip at 150 THB  (IMPACT Arena Muang Thong Thani)
Shuttle bus ticket from/ to event venue at 200THB at Bangkok Tourist

- Large baggage or traveling suitcases are not allowed at the concert hall. As there is no baggage deposit counter at the venue, you can store your belongings at our secure luggage locker at Bangkok Tourist Lounge, 1 st floor @SHOW DC BANGKOK on Rama 9 Road.
- Free luggage deposit service valid only 12 hours/ usage/person from 10.00 – 00.00 hrs.(midnight)
- Free 1 time shower room service at Bangkok Tourist Lounge from 10.00 – 00.00 hrs.(midnight)


Reserve shuttle bus ticket Lounge at SHOW DC Rama 9 and all Thaiticketmajor Counter or www.thaiticketmajor.com

Click here to reserve : https://goo.gl/KR55m3

Operating hours: 10.00 – 22.00 hrs. daily

Special for customers who reserve shuttle bus ticket, Get free parking for 16 hr. @ SHOW DC BANGKOK (Parking service from 10.00 -03.00 hrs.)
