SHOW DC Deliciously Yours
Indulge with variety of food with over 1,000 menus at Asian Food District, 5th Floor, SHOW DC

Weekday Promotion (Mon – Fri, 11.00 – 14.00 and 17.00 – 20.00 Hrs.)
Enjoy the special set menu, starting from 99 THB. Choose your favorite dish from  various restaurants at the happy hour time. 

Weekend Promotion
Every Saturday and Sunday, spend from 500 THB at the participating restaurants, get Asian Food District cash voucher worth 500 THB to enjoy a variety of dish and sweets at the participating restaurants. 

List of Participating Restaurants
1. Celeb E-San (Open on 24/02/2017)
2. Cheeseaholic
3. Diamond Restaurant
4. Huck Ice Cream
5. Incheon Chicken Wings
6. Krua Wandee (Only Weekend Promotion)
7. Lagoon
8. Mahi
9. Mini Me Pot by Ok Shabu Shabu
10. Monkey Shake
11. Pam Pam Bingsu
12. Siam Royale
13. Sweet Planet
14. Tanavakatsu
15. TomyumSter
16. Wang Seong
17. Yamanashi
18. YUE KEE 
19. Mango Bar 
Now – 31 March 2017

• Present the receipt worth more than 500 THB (cannot combine the receipt) from the participating restaurants in Asian Food Districts on 5th Floor, get one 500 THB worth Asian Food District   Voucher to use at any of the participating restaurants. Limited 1 person/voucher/day
