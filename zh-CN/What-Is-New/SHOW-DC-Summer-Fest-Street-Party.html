SHOW DC SUMMER FEST STREET PARTY

Share
 

Want to be part of our "Street Party" on 11th April and watch the hottest K-Pop artist concert? Ticket is not for sale! All you have to do is just shop, eat, and enjoy at SHOW DC, and get the free ticket! Here’s the deal.

Every time you shop, please make sure to show your SHOW ID on SHOW DC mobile Application for the counter staff.

*If you don’t have SHOW ID, just download the SHOW DC Mobile Application and register here! 


           

 

VIP Stand and Regular Stand Tickets 

  • VIP Stand Ticket : Collect receipt from any stores in SHOW DC worth more than 5,000 THB, get 1 prize of 2 VIP Stand Ticket.
  • Regular Ticket : Collect receipt from any stores in SHOW DC worth more than 2,000 THB, or only 1,000 THB from the SHOWCARD/SHOW ID participating stores (Notice the SHOWCARD signage in front or inside the store), get 1 prize of Regular tickets. Customer must always present SHOWCARD or SHOW ID on app to the cashier to scan before making a payment.
  • Collect receipt from 27 Mar – 9 Apr 2017 only.
  • Receipt which has been used for redemption, cannot be reused in other promotion campaign.
  • Receipt from Food Tradition cannot be used in this activity.
  • Limited 1 prize (2 tickets)/1 customer (with the same name, surname, and ID card) only.
  • Concert tickets can be redeemed during 1– 9 Apr 2017 at Thai Ticket Major Counter from 10.00 – 22.00 at the Tourist Lounge, 1st Floor. Customer need to present ID card, SHOW ID, and original receipts to redeem for the ticket. 
  • Limited quota of VIP Stand ticket redemption of 1 – 8 Apr at 100 prizes per day, and at 92 prizes on 9 Apr 2017.
  • Limited quota of Regular Stand ticket redemption of 1– 8 Apr at 275 prizes per day, and at 200 prizes on 9 Apr 2017.
  • In case the total number of tickets redemption has not reached the quota each day, the leftover will be combined to the next day.
  • SHOW DC reserves the right to change the condition without prior notice.
  • Customer participating in this activity have read and well understood, and agrees to all the terms and conditions mentioned above.
  • Staff and their family (spouse/parents/ siblings/ or children) of SHOW DC and its subsidiaries are not allowed to participate in this activity.

 

Exclusive Prize For 8 Top Spenders Tickets 

  •  Get to meet & greet, and take a group photos with each of the 4 Korean artists, as well as 2 Street Party VIP Stand tickets. 

  • Calculated only the spending at the SHOWCARD/SHOW ID participating stores. Customer needs to present SHOWCARD/SHOW ID on App to the cashier every time before making a payment. 
  • Collect and combine the spending occurred during 28 Mar – 9 Apr 2017.
  • Customer who would like to participate in this activity must register with the full name, mobile number, and SHOW ID number on the 9th April, 2017 at Redemption Counter, 1st Floor. 
  • SHOW DC will calculate the 8 top spenders from the SHOWCARD system
  • The 8 top spenders will be announced on SHOW DC Facebook and www.showdc.co.th on 10 Apr 2017 at 2PM. 
  • Spending from Food Tradition cannot be used in this promotion. 
  • SHOW DC reserves the right to change the condition without prior notice. 
  • Customer participating in this activity have read and well understood, and agrees to all the terms and conditions mentioned above. 
  • Staff and their family (spouse/parents/ siblings/ or children) of SHOW DC and its subsidiaries are not allowed to participate in this activity. 

 

Lucky Winner Shopping Tickets 

  • Shop every 500 THB, get a chance to win a prize of 2 VIP Stand tickets. 
  • Shop every 200 THB, get a chance to win a prize of 2 Regular tickets. 
  • Collect receipt from any store in SHOW DC, from 28 Mar – 2 Apr 2017 only.
  • Receipts can be redeemed for lucky draw coupon on the same day of purchase only.
  • Receipts from Food Tradition cannot be used in this campaign
  • Written Cash Receipt from any stores cannot be used in this campaign
  • Receipt can be redeemed for a lucky draw coupon from 28 Mar – 2 Apr 2017 at the Redemption Counter (limited only 10 coupons/person/day)
  • Limited 1 prize (2 tickets)/1 customer (with the same name, surname, and ID card) only.
  • Receipt which has been used for redemption, cannot be reused in other promotion campaign. 
  • Lucky draw for VIP Stand ticket will be conducted every day, 40 winners per day (2 tickets each) from 28 Mar – 1 Apr, 2017, and 50 winners (2 tickets each) on 2 Apr 2017. 
  • Lucky draw for Regular Stand ticket will be conducted every day, 80 winners per day (2 tickets each) from 28 Mar – 1 Apr, 2017, and 100 winners (2 tickets each) on 2 Apr 2017. 
  • Lucky Draw Winners will be announced on SHOW DC Facebook and www.showdc.co.th on the next day at 11am.
  • The lucky draw winner can get the party ticket from 1 – 9 Apr, 2017, 10.00 – 22.00 at ThaiTicketMajor counter at the Tourist Lounge, 1st Floor only. 
  • The lucky draw winner must present the original and copy of their ID card to claim for the tickets. Otherwise customer shall be forfeited. 
  • SHOW DC reserves the right to change the condition without prior notice. 
  • Customer participating in this activity have read and well understood, and agrees to all the terms and conditions mentioned above. 
  • Staff and their family (spouse/parents/ siblings/ or children) of SHOW DC and its subsidiaries are not allowed to participate in this activity. 

 

Get A Chance To The Red Carpet Zone Tickets

  • Shop on the event day (11 Apr 2017) and show the receipt worth 200THB or more.
  • Receipts can be from any store in SHOW DC and can be combined. 
  • Limited only the first 500 people
  • Receipts cannot be reused.
  • Receipt from Food Tradition cannot be used.
  • SHOW DC reserves the right to change the condition without prior notice. 
  • Customer participating in this activity have read and well understood, and agrees to all the terms and conditions mentioned above. 
  • Staff and their family (spouse/parents/ siblings/ or children) of SHOW DC and its subsidiaries are not allowed to participate in this activity. 

 

Red Carpet Zone

  • Distribute the queue card for Red Carpet Zone at 10 AM at Atrium Zone  (First come first served basis)
  • Redeem for wristband, and choose the zone (A,B,C,D) where you want to be at 3 PM, as per the queue number. 
  • Showtime starts at 5 PM.

 

TOP SPENDER

  • Apply for Top Spender campaign at the Redemption Counter, Tourist Lounge Wing, at 10 am. onwards. 
  • Present your original ID card or Passport, together with SHOW ID in SHOW DC application at Redemption Counter. Name/surname of SHOW ID and original ID Card/Passport must be the same.
  • Announcement of 8 Top Spenders will be on 10th April, 2017 at 2 pm.
  • 8 Top spenders must show up and register themselves on 11th April, 2017 at 4 PM at Atrium Zone, and standby for meet&greet with the artists.
