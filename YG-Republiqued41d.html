BANGKOK'S NEWEST ADDITION TO IT'S VIBRANT ENTERTAINMENT SCENE
Discover the exciting world of entertainment

YG République sums up a fulfilling night out with great food, good drink and glorious time on the dance floor. Bringing together everyone’s favorite Seoul rooftop bar experience, a mouth-watering selection of Korean BBQ and munchies, and an eye-candy range of signature cocktails and popular drinks, your fabulous after-work revelry gives you a taste of all-in-one Korean clubbing & dining experience with a breathtaking rooftop view.

Australian style coffee and hearty meals in a Seoulful setting, 3 Birds café is a place for you to enjoy a barista-blessed drinks menu of short blacks served with cream and flat whites with a velvety milk foam on top. As well as an all-day menu of a range of healthy and tasty meals such as the avocado and shrimp sandwich, kale and quinoa salad and others, you can take the time to peruse a big selection of YG merchandise featuring all your favourite Korean stars at All That YG.

Operating hours:

10:00 - 22:00

Signature Dishes

The 3 Butchers in Hongdae is where YG Entertainment first started their food empire born from their famous canteen. This brand-new edition of their barbeque restaurant combines hip Korean music culture with the finest cuts of pork, barbequed to perfection. The décor reflects the YG aesthetic with an open kitchen and “Big Bang” style graffiti adorning the walls. Every detail is important from the quality of the meat to the temperature of the barbeque, which is checked regularly by the waiters. Accompanying dishes such as Mok Sal (Pork Neck)and Samgyeopsal (Pork Belly) are three very special sauces for dipping.

Operating hours:

10:00 - 22:00

Signature Dishes

Hongdae is the centre of Seoul nightlife and the Korean pubs are at the heart of Hongdae. Experience this unique music and food culture at K-Pub, brought to you by the YG entertainment team behind some of Korean music’s biggest names. Psy, Big Bang, 2NE1 and other YG artwork, music and videos are on display amongst the chic decor. The menu features a selections of snacks and pub favourites and a barbeque platter with a variety of meats, fried shrimp and squid, all washed down with a range of beverages including the house YG Beer.

Operating hours:

10:00 - 22:00

Signature Dishes

YG Entertainment has become more than just a music label. Through the guidance of founder, Yang Hyun-suk, they have branched out into the restaurant business thanks to the popularity of the canteen at the YG office where local stars and international names such as Justin Bieber and Will Smith came to eat. YG Club is their vision of the club environment and a satellite of the Hongdae experience with a soundtrack of Big Bang, 2NE1, G-Dragon, Taeyang, iKon, Psy, and other YG stars.

Operating hours:

10:00 - 22:00
